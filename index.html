About

Hello there! My name is George Chelal Chementer, and I am a 23-year-old Kenyan with a deep-rooted passion for technology. From a young age, I found myself fascinated by how gadgets and computers work, which led me to experiment with different tech-related projects. Over the years, I've attended workshops, online courses, and hackathons to broaden my skill set beyond the classroom.

My journey has been filled with challenges and successes that have shaped my understanding of the tech world. Whether it's tinkering with electronics, learning a new programming language, or simply staying up-to-date with the latest trends, I always strive to push my boundaries and learn something new every day. This curiosity drives me forward, and I'm excited to see where it takes me next.

Education

My educational journey began at Carmelvale Catholic Primary School, where I studied for eight years and completed my primary education in 2015. During this time, I not only developed strong foundational skills but also discovered my growing interest in science and technology.

After primary school, I joined Sunshine Secondary School from 2016 to 2019. Here, I had the opportunity to participate in various clubs and competitions that further fueled my passion for electronics and innovation. I particularly enjoyed science fairs and coding clubs, where I collaborated with like-minded peers.

Currently, I am pursuing a degree in Electrical Engineering at the University of Nairobi. This program allows me to delve deeper into the world of circuits, power systems, and embedded technology. I am constantly amazed by the synergy between hardware and software, and I hope to use my education to create impactful solutions that bridge these two domains.
